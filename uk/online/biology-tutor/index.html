06 / Questions

Online Biology tuition FAQs.

Is the assessment really free?

Yes. The Biology assessment is free, with no payment and no obligation. You only decide about paid tuition afterwards, once you have a clear picture of where your child is losing marks.

What if I do not know what my child is struggling with?

That is exactly what the assessment is for. You do not need to diagnose the problem. You only need to know your child is stuck — we find the specific gap and explain it clearly.

How does online tuition work?

Lessons are live online using a shared screen and digital whiteboard. The assessment and tuition direction are teacher-led and guided by Mr G Dabhad, QTS, and lessons are delivered by our DBS-checked tutors. You receive a link before each session.

What ages and stages do you cover?

We teach Biology from Year 7 through Year 13 — secondary (KS3 science with a biology focus), GCSE (Years 10–11), and A-Level (Years 12–13).

Is online Biology tuition suitable for a child who lacks confidence?

Yes. Many pupils come to us having lost confidence. Because our online Biology tutoring assesses first and teaches from the right starting point, lessons feel achievable rather than overwhelming, which rebuilds confidence over time.

How much does online Biology tuition cost?

Year 7 to GCSE: £14.50 per hour in a small group (4–5 pupils), £22.50 in a trio (3 pupils), or £39.50 one-to-one. A-Level (Sixth Form): £18.50 per hour in a group of 4–5, or £45 one-to-one. We recommend the right format after the assessment. The assessment itself is always free.

Who leads the assessment and tuition plan?

The assessment and tuition plan are teacher-led and guided by Mr G Dabhad, QTS, founder of Improve Tuition since 2007. Lessons are delivered by DBS-checked tutors working to that plan.

Which exam boards and curricula do you cover for GCSE and A-Level Biology?

We cover the main UK exam boards, including AQA, Edexcel, OCR and WJEC/Eduqas, for Biology. At GCSE this includes separate Biology and the biology content within Combined Science (Trilogy and Synergy). We also teach IGCSE (Cambridge and Edexcel) and IB Diploma Biology for international and expat families. Tuition is matched to your child's board and tier so practice questions and required practicals reflect the papers they will actually sit.

Does online Biology tuition actually work?

Yes. Lessons are live and interactive, using a shared screen and digital whiteboard so your child works through problems with the tutor in real time, just as they would in person. Because we assess first and teach from the right starting point, online tutoring stays focused on the specific gaps holding your child back.

Do you teach international and expat families outside the UK?

Yes. Because lessons are live online, we teach UK families nationwide and expat and international-school families worldwide — including Dubai, Qatar, Singapore and Hong Kong. We cover UK and international curricula (GCSE, IGCSE, A-Level and IB), and lessons can be arranged around your local time zone.

Do you offer Biology tuition near me in Batley?

Yes. As well as teaching online across the UK, we offer in-person Biology tuition at our Batley centre on George Street, serving families across Dewsbury, Birstall, Heckmondwike and the wider Kirklees area. The free assessment is the same whether you choose online or in-person lessons. If you're searching for a Biology tutor near you or local Biology tutoring in the Batley area, this is the same teacher-led service either way.
