02 / What May Be Happening

Same low grade. Different cause.

You probably know your child is losing marks. The harder question is why — and the answer is rarely “not trying hard enough.”

  • They revise hard but the same kind of question keeps costing them marks — paper after paper.
  • They know the content but freeze on extended writing, source analysis or case-study response questions.
  • They run out of time in mocks, even when they knew the material when they sat down.

Which one is your child? You won’t know for sure until we look. That’s what the free assessment is for.

06 / Questions

The questions parents ask most.

Which IGCSE subjects do you tutor?

Maths, English Language, English Literature, Combined Science (Co-ordinated), Triple Science (separate Biology, Chemistry and Physics), Business Studies, Economics, Religious Studies, History and Geography. Other subjects on request — just ask when you enquire.

Which IGCSE exam boards do you cover?

Cambridge International (CAIE), Pearson Edexcel, AQA International, and Oxford AQA. We confirm your child’s exam board at the free assessment, then tailor lessons to the specific specification.

What’s the difference between IGCSE and GCSE?

IGCSE is the international version of GCSE, used by British-curriculum schools worldwide and some UK independent schools. Content overlap is high, but exam formats and certain topics differ. We teach to the specific IGCSE specification your child sits.

Can lessons work across time zones?

Yes. We tutor families across the UK, the Middle East and Asia — including Dubai, Singapore and Hong Kong. Lessons are scheduled to suit your child’s local time.

Is the IGCSE assessment really free?

Yes. No payment to book, no obligation to continue. We find what’s costing marks, explain the next step — that’s it.

How do lessons work?

Lessons follow the assessment plan, not a generic past-paper pack. A qualified teacher guides the plan; our DBS-checked IGCSE tutors deliver each lesson. Pace is set by understanding, not a timetable.

How much does IGCSE tuition cost?

From £14.50/hr in a group of 4–5 · £22.50/hr small group of 2–3 · £39.50/hr one-to-one. Exact rate confirmed after the free assessment.
