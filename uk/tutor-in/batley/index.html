How we teach

We don't guess. We assess, then teach the gap.

Most struggles come from one or two missing foundations further back. Find those, fix those, and confidence returns fast. That's the whole method.

1

Find the gap

A free assessment pinpoints exactly which topics and foundations are missing — not a vague level, a specific list.

2

Teach it clearly

A qualified teacher works through each gap with a clear plan, the right pace, and methods that match how your child learns.

3

Track the progress

Progress is monitored carefully so you can see the gaps closing and the marks moving in the right direction.
