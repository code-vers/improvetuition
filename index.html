Common questions

Three things parents ask first.

  • What happens at the free assessment?

    We listen first. You tell us what's going on — the school worry, the slipping grade, the homework battles. We work out where your child is actually getting stuck and tell you honestly whether tuition would help, and which type. No pitch. You'll speak to a qualified teacher.

  • Online or in-person — which is better for my child?

    Both work. Online suits busy weeks, families outside Batley, and pupils comfortable on screens — and works surprisingly well for younger pupils too. In-person at our Batley centre suits primary children who need eye contact and structure, and pupils who lose focus on screens. We'll suggest which after the assessment.

  • What if it isn't working?

    Tell us. There are no contracts — you pay monthly in advance and you can stop any time. More often we'll change the tutor, change the approach, or honestly tell you tuition isn't the right answer. We'd rather lose a customer than waste a family's time.
