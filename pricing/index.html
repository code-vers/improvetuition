Pricing

Tuition from £14.50 per hour.

Most of our pupils learn in small group classes — typically four to five children, with full teacher attention. Smaller groups and 1-to-1 sessions are also available. Same prices online or at our Batley centre.

All lessons are 1 hour. For Year 2 pupils, lessons are 30 minutes (priced pro rata). Same prices at our Batley centre or online.

What's included

Every parent gets the same thing. A free first assessment for any pupil up to GCSE, so we understand where your child is before tuition begins. Frequent progress feedback by message — usually WhatsApp — so you always know how lessons are going. We get in touch directly when there's something to share, whether it's strong progress or a concern that needs your attention.

There is no registration fee, no exam-period surcharge, and no minimum commitment. We ask families to bring their child's school books and any past papers or revision guides — we'll work from those, since it keeps tuition aligned with what's happening in school.

How payment works

Lessons are paid monthly in advance. There is no long contract — you can stop any time. We invoice once a month and you're never tied in beyond the month you've paid for.
