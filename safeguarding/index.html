Safeguarding

How we protect the children we teach.

At Improve Tuition, pupils are taught by DBS-checked tutors and supported through clear safeguarding expectations. Whether lessons take place at our Batley centre or online, we aim to provide a calm, responsible and professional learning environment.

Improve Tuition is led by Mr G Dabhad, a Qualified Teacher (QTS) with eighteen years of experience. He oversees the academic direction of the service and expects every tutor to work safely, respectfully and appropriately with pupils.

Parents can contact us directly if they have a concern, question or safeguarding matter they wish to raise. Safeguarding is not an extra feature. It is part of responsible tuition.
